Standing tall at 112 feet, the magnificent Adiyogi statue represents the 112 ways one can attain liberation. Recognized by the Guinness World Records, this spiritual sanctuary crafted by Sadhguru draws seekers from globally.

Surrounded by the lush greenery of the Western Ghats, the profound atmosphere here offers an unparalleled sense of calm and rejuvenation. Whether you are observing the powerful evening projection shows or simply sitting in quiet contemplation, the vibrance of this space is truly unforgettable.
